## Overview

The **Reports** module gives you data-driven insights into your church's health — giving trends, attendance patterns, member demographics, engagement levels, and more. Everything is visualised with charts and can be exported for board meetings, audits, or leadership reviews.

Go to **Reports** in the sidebar.

## Report Sections

| Section | What It Covers |
|---|---|
| 📊 **Overview** | Giving summary, attendance trends, member growth, top givers |
| 👥 **Demographics** | Age, gender, location, occupation, marital status |
| 💓 **Engagement** | Attendance consistency, at-risk members, visitor conversion, retention |
| 💰 **Giving Intelligence** | First-time givers, lapsed givers, giving consistency |
| 🔧 **Custom Report Builder** | Build and save your own filtered reports |

> 💡 Demographics, Engagement, Giving Intelligence, and the Report Builder require the **Growing plan** or higher.

---

## 1. Overview Report

### Period Selector
Choose: This Week / This Month / This Quarter / This Year / Custom date range

### What You See

- **Giving Summary** — total amount, number of transactions, % change vs previous period, breakdown by category and payment method
- **Attendance Summary** — total attendance, services held, average per service, first-timer count
- **Member Growth Chart** — monthly line chart of membership growth over 6 months
- **Giving Trend Chart** — 6-month bar chart of total giving per month
- **Attendance by Service Type** — average attendance tiles for Sunday, Midweek, Youth, Prayer, and Special services
- **Top Givers** — top 5 giving members for the selected period

### Exporting

Click **Export** → choose **PDF** (formatted with your logo) or **CSV** (raw data for spreadsheets).

## 2. Demographics Report

Understand who your congregation is:

| Chart | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Age Distribution** | Breakdown by age group (0–18, 19–30, 31–45, 46–60, 60+) |
| **Gender Distribution** | Male / Female / Not specified |
| **Marital Status** | Single / Married / Divorced / Widowed |
| **Location** | Which cities/regions your members come from |
| **Occupation** | Top occupations in your congregation |
| **Member Status** | Visitor / New Convert / Active / Full Member / Inactive split |
| **Join Trend** | When members joined — month by month |

---

## 3. Engagement Report

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|---|---|
| **Attendance Consistency** | % of members who attend every week, most weeks, occasionally, rarely |
| **Visitor Conversion Rate** | % of visitors in the last 90 days who became active members |
| **New Member Retention** | % of members who joined in the last 6 months still attending regularly |
| **At-Risk Members** | Active members who haven't attended in 3+ weeks |

Click **Create Task** next to any at-risk member to immediately assign a pastoral follow-up task.

## 4. Giving Intelligence

| Report | What It Helps With |
|---|---|
| **First-Time Givers** | Members who gave for the first time this month — send a personal thank-you |
| **Lapsed Givers** | Members who gave before but haven't given in 90+ days — gentle stewardship follow-up |
| **Giving Consistency** | % of congregation that gives weekly, monthly, occasionally, never |
| **Occupation vs Giving** | Which occupations give the most — informs stewardship campaigns |

---

## 5. Custom Report Builder

Build custom reports from scratch:

1. **Choose a Report Type** — Members, Giving, Attendance, Youth, Converts, Events, or Groups
2. **Select Columns** — pick exactly which fields to include
3. **Add Filters** — e.g. Status = Active, Join Date between Jan–Mar 2026
4. **Sort** — choose a column and direction
5. **Run Report** — preview the data

Then **Export CSV**, **Export PDF**, or **Save Report** to reuse later.
