## Overview

The **Giving** module lets you record, track, and report on all financial contributions to your church — cash offerings, Mobile Money, card payments, cheques, and bank transfers. Every giving record is tied to a member profile, a category, and a date.

Go to **Giving** in the sidebar.

## Setting Up Offering Categories

Before recording offerings, set up the categories your church uses (Tithes, Sunday Offering, Building Fund, Welfare Fund, Mission Offering, etc.):

1. Go to **Settings → Church Profile**
2. Scroll to **Offering Categories** → click **+ Add Category**
3. Enter the name and save

---

## Recording an Offering (Manual Entry)

For cash, cheque, or any offline payment:

1. Go to **Giving** → click **+ Record Offering**
2. Fill in:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Member** | Search by name or phone (leave blank for anonymous) |
| **Amount** | The amount given |
| **Currency** | Defaults to your church currency |
| **Payment Method** | Cash, Cheque, MoMo, Card, Bank Transfer |
| **Category** | Tithe, Sunday Offering, etc. |
| **Date** | Defaults to today |
| **Notes** | Optional (e.g. "Thanksgiving for child's graduation") |
| **Anonymous** | Tick if the member doesn't want their name on the record |

3. Click **Save**

> 💡 After recording, Shepherd can print a **giving receipt** for the member.

## Collecting via Card or Mobile Money (Live Payments)

For real-time card or MoMo collection:

1. Go to **Giving → Collect**
2. Search and select the member
3. Enter the amount and category
4. Choose **Card** or **Mobile Money**
5. For MoMo: enter the member's number — Shepherd auto-detects the network (MTN/AirtelTigo/Telecel) from the phone prefix
6. Click **Collect** — a Paystack payment popup appears
7. Member completes the payment → offering is recorded automatically

> 💡 **Payout setup required:** Card and MoMo collection requires completing payout setup in **Settings → Payouts**.

## WhatsApp Giving (GIVE Command)

Members can give directly via WhatsApp:

1. Member sends `GIVE 50` to your church's WhatsApp number
2. Shepherd asks them to choose a category
3. Member confirms → Shepherd charges their registered MoMo via Paystack
4. Giving is recorded automatically — no staff action needed

This works 24/7 and is the most convenient method for regular givers.

## Giving Receipts

In the Recent Offerings list, click **🧾 Receipt** next to any offering. A printable receipt opens with member name, amount, category, payment method, date, transaction reference, and your church receipt prefix (e.g. "GCI-00045").

---

## Member Giving Statements

1. Go to **Giving** → search for or click a member's name
2. Their **Giving Statement** shows all giving records with totals
3. Click **Print** for a printable version

Useful for annual tithe statements, pastoral conversations, and member self-service requests.

---

## Donor Insights

| Insight | What It Means |
|---|---|
| **First-time givers this month** | Members who gave for the first time this month |
| **Lapsed givers** | Members who gave before but haven't given in 90+ days |
| **Recent givers** | Members who gave in the last 24 hours |

---

## Exporting Giving Data

Go to **Giving** → click **Export**. A CSV file downloads with all records including member name, amount, category, method, date, and transaction reference.

---

## Paystack Sub-accounts (Giving Payouts)

To receive card and MoMo giving directly into your church's account:

1. Go to **Settings → Payouts** → click **Set Up Payouts**
2. Enter your bank account or MoMo details
3. Shepherd registers a Paystack Sub-account for your church
4. All collected giving flows directly to your account

Shepherd charges a **2% platform fee** on all digital giving.

## Tips

- ✅ **Set up your offering categories first** before recording any giving
- ✅ **Complete payout setup** before enabling card/MoMo collection
- ✅ **Use the GIVE WhatsApp command** for regular givers — convenient and automatic
- ✅ **Export monthly** for your accounting records
- ✅ **Check donor insights** — follow up with lapsed givers through your stewardship team
