## Overview

**Groups** in Shepherd represent any sub-community within your church — cell groups, ministries, committees, choirs, prayer teams, Bible study groups, and more. You can organise members into groups, assign leaders, record meeting notes, and send targeted broadcasts to specific groups.

## Group Types

| Type | Typical Use |
|---|---|
| **Small Group** | Neighbourhood or home fellowship groups |
| **Cell Group** | Cell church structure units |
| **Ministry** | Worship team, media, ushers, etc. |
| **Committee** | Finance committee, building committee, etc. |
| **Choir** | Music/choir groups |
| **Prayer** | Intercessory prayer groups |
| **Bible Study** | Weekly or periodic Bible study groups |
| **Youth** | Youth-specific groups |
| **Women** | Women's fellowship |
| **Men** | Men's fellowship |
| **Other** | Any other type |

## Creating a Group

1. Go to **Groups** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ Create Group**
3. Fill in the details:

**Required:**
- **Group Name** — e.g. "Adabraka Cell Group A", "Media Ministry"

**Optional but recommended:**
- **Group Type** — select from the list above
- **Description** — brief description of the group's purpose
- **Leader** — assign a member as the group leader
- **Meeting Day** — which day the group meets
- **Meeting Time** — e.g. 18:00
- **Meeting Location** — e.g. "Pastor John's house" or "Room 3"
- **Branch** *(multi-branch churches)* — which branch this group belongs to

4. Click **Save**

## Managing Group Members

### Adding Members to a Group

1. Open the group → click **+ Add Member**
2. Search by name and select the member
3. Optionally assign a **role** within the group (e.g. "Assistant Leader", "Secretary")
4. Click **Add**

### Removing a Member

Click **Remove** next to the member → confirm. The member is not deleted from your database — just removed from this group.

---

## Recording Group Meetings

1. Open the group → click **Record Meeting**
2. Fill in:
   - **Meeting Date**
   - **Topic / Theme** — e.g. "The Armour of God"
   - **Notes** — summary of what happened
   - **Attendee Count** and **Absent Count**
   - **New Visitors** — first-timers at this meeting
3. Click **Save Meeting**

Meeting history is stored and visible on the group's detail page.

---

## Sending Messages to a Group

1. Go to **Messages → Broadcasts** → click **New Broadcast**
2. Under **Recipients**, choose **Group**
3. Select the group from the dropdown
4. Write your message and send via WhatsApp or SMS

> 💡 Only members with a valid phone number will receive the message.

---

## Tips for Using Groups Effectively

- ✅ **Create a group for every ministry** — even small ones. It makes targeted communication much easier.
- ✅ **Assign group leaders** — leaders show on the group card and can be referenced in task assignments
- ✅ **Record meetings regularly** — it builds a history of your church's small group activity
- ✅ **Use groups for broadcast targeting** — instead of messaging everyone, message just the choir or just the ushers
- ✅ **Branch-scope your groups** — in multi-branch churches, assign each group to its branch
