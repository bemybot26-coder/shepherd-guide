## Overview

The **Youth Ministry** module gives your church a dedicated space to track, care for, and engage your young people — from crèche right up to tertiary level and young working adults. It includes educational stages, school tracking, parent links, engagement scores, school visit logs, and youth events.

> 💡 **Plan requirement:** Youth tracking requires the **Flourishing plan** or higher.

Go to **Youth Ministry** in the sidebar.

## Educational Stages

| Stage | Typical Age Range |
|---|---|
| **Crèche** | 0–2 years |
| **Nursery** | 3–5 years |
| **Primary** | 6–11 years (Basic 1–6) |
| **JHS** | 12–15 years (JHS 1–3) |
| **SHS** | 16–18 years (SHS 1–3) |
| **Tertiary** | 18–24 years (University/Polytechnic) |
| **Working** | Young adults who have entered the workforce |

## The Youth Dashboard

| Stat | What It Shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Youth** | Total registered youth members |
| **By Stage** | Breakdown by Crèche, Primary, JHS, SHS, Tertiary, Working |
| **At Risk** 🔴 | Youth who haven't attended in 3+ weeks |
| **Needs Visit** | Youth flagged for a pastoral school/home visit |

At-risk youth are listed prominently at the top so your team can act immediately.

---

## Engagement Score

Shepherd automatically calculates an **engagement score** (0–100) for each youth based on recent attendance patterns. A youth is automatically flagged **At Risk** (🔴) when they haven't attended in 3 or more weeks.

---

## Adding a Youth Member

Youth must first exist as a regular member. Then create their youth profile:

1. Go to **Youth Ministry** → click **+ Add Youth**
2. Search for the member by name
3. Fill in their youth-specific details:
   - **Educational Stage**
   - **School** — select from your registered schools
   - **Grade/Form Level** — e.g. "JHS 2", "Level 200"
   - **Stream** — e.g. "Science", "Business" (for SHS/Tertiary)
   - **Expected Graduation Year**
   - **Notes**
4. Click **Save**

---

## Youth Profile

Each youth has a detailed profile with:
- **Overview** — educational stage, school, grade, engagement score, at-risk status
- **Attendance** — full attendance history with engagement trend
- **Parent/Guardian** — links to parent members in the database
- **Visits** — school and home visit logs
- **Tasks** — follow-up tasks related to this youth

---

## School Visit Logs

Track pastoral visits to schools (for JHS/SHS youth especially):

1. Open a youth member's profile → click **Log School Visit**
2. Enter: School name, date, notes, outcome
3. Save

School visit history appears on the youth profile and in the overall Youth Ministry reports.

---

## Tips

- ✅ **Review the At-Risk list weekly** — 3 missed services is early enough to reach out before someone drifts away
- ✅ **Link parents to their children** — it helps when you need to contact the family
- ✅ **Record school visits** — it demonstrates pastoral commitment and builds trust with families
- ✅ **Use Notes for school-specific context** — e.g. "Writing WASSCE in May 2026 — needs prayer support"
