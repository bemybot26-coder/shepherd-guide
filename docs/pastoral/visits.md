## Overview

The **Visits** section tracks all pastoral visits your team makes — home visits, hospital visits, bereavements, and outreach — and surfaces **member-requested visits** from WhatsApp so nothing gets missed.

Access visits at **Pastoral Care → Visits** or directly from the **Visits** sidebar link.

## How Visit Requests Come In

### 1. Member Requests via WhatsApp

When a member sends `VISIT` to your church WhatsApp number:
1. The bot asks what type of visit they need (Home / Hospital / Other)
2. The member provides a brief reason
3. The request is logged in the Visits dashboard with a **🙋 Requested** badge
4. All pastoral staff receive a WhatsApp notification immediately

### 2. Staff Creates a Visit Manually

Staff can log a visit directly:
1. Go to **Pastoral Care → Visits** → click **+ Log a Visit**
2. Fill in the visit details (see below)
3. Click **Log Visit**

---

## Visit Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🙋 **Requested** | Member asked for a visit — not yet assigned to staff |
| ✅ **Assigned** | A staff member has been assigned to make the visit |
| *(completed)* | Visit has been logged by staff with notes |

Pending visit requests are shown prominently at the top of the Visits tab with a count badge.

---

## Logging a Visit

1. Go to **Pastoral Care → Visits** → click **+ Log a Visit**
2. Fill in:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| **Member** | Who was visited (optional for outreach) |
| **Visited By** | Which staff member made the visit |
| **Visit Type** | Home / Hospital / Bereavement / Outreach / Other |
| **Date** | Defaults to today |
| **Location** | Address or hospital name (optional) |
| **Notes** | What happened during the visit |
| **Create a follow-up task** | Tick to create a follow-up task in the same action |

3. Click **Log Visit**

### Creating a Follow-Up Task at the Same Time

If the visit reveals something that needs follow-up, tick **"Create a follow-up task"** before saving. A task sub-form appears — fill in the title, assignee, and due date. Both the visit log and the task are created in one action. The assigned staff member receives a WhatsApp notification immediately.

## Visit Types

| Type | Use Case |
|---|---|
| 🏠 Home | Visiting a member at their home |
| 🏥 Hospital | Visiting a sick or admitted member |
| 💜 Bereavement | Supporting a grieving family |
| 🌍 Outreach | Community or evangelism visit |
| 📋 Other | Any other type of visit |

## Member Visit History

To see all visits for a specific member:
1. Go to **Members** → open the member's profile
2. Scroll to the **Visits** section
3. See every visit logged for this member with date, type, staff, and notes

---

## Tips

- ✅ **Log visits the same day** — notes are more accurate when fresh
- ✅ **Always create a follow-up task** if the member needs continued support
- ✅ **Assign requested visits quickly** — members who ask for a visit are often in an urgent situation
- ✅ **Use the Bereavement type for family losses** — it links to the Care Cases system for automatic follow-up
