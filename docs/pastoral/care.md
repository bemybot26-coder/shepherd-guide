## Overview

The **Pastoral Care** module is where your church's shepherding work happens. It brings together prayer requests, counselling appointments, follow-up tasks, member feedback, pastor availability, and visit logs — all in one place so no one falls through the cracks.

Go to **Pastoral Care** in the sidebar to access it.

## 1. Prayer Requests

Members submit prayer requests via WhatsApp (`PRAY` command) or staff adds them in the dashboard.

### Prayer Request Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Open** | Newly submitted, not yet attended to |
| **Praying** | The pastoral team is actively praying |
| **Answered** | The prayer has been answered 🎉 |
| **Closed** | Request closed without a specific answer |

### Updating Prayer Status

1. Find the prayer request
2. Click **Mark as Praying** or **Mark as Answered**
3. Shepherd automatically sends a WhatsApp notification to the member in their preferred language:
   - *Praying:* "Hi [Name], our pastoral team is praying for your request. God hears you! 🙏"
   - *Answered:* "We're rejoicing with you! Your prayer request has been marked as answered! 🎉"

### Assigning a Prayer Request

1. Click **Assign** on the prayer request card
2. Search and select a staff member
3. They receive a WhatsApp notification immediately

> **Privacy note:** For anonymous requests, the WhatsApp notification says *"A member has submitted a confidential prayer request"* — the actual text is never revealed.

### Broadcasting a Prayer Request

If a member allowed their request to be shared:
1. Open the prayer request → click **📢 Share with Church**
2. Shepherd broadcasts it to all members via WhatsApp
3. The broadcast appears in **Messages → Broadcasts** with a 🙏 amber badge — read-only

## 2. Appointments

Members can request pastoral appointments via WhatsApp (`BOOK` command) or staff can create them manually.

### Appointment Types
Counselling, Prayer Session, New Member Orientation, Financial Counselling, Pre-marital Counselling, Visitation, General Meeting, Other

### Appointment Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Requested, not yet confirmed |
| **Confirmed** | Pastor has confirmed the appointment |
| **Completed** | The meeting took place |
| **Cancelled** | Appointment was cancelled |

### Confirming an Appointment

1. Go to **Pastoral Care → Appointments**
2. Find the pending appointment → click **Confirm**
3. Optionally set a date and time
4. Shepherd sends a personalised WhatsApp to the member, signed with the pastor's first name

> **Tip:** The confirmation uses "I" (first person) and the pastor's name — much more personal than a generic team reply.

## 3. Follow-Up Tasks

Tasks are action items for your pastoral team. They can be created manually or auto-generated by Journey stage templates.

### Creating a Task

From any prayer request, appointment, or visit card:
1. Click **+ Add Task**
2. Fill in title, assignee, due date, and optional notes
3. Click **Create Task**

The assigned staff member receives a WhatsApp notification immediately with the member's name, phone number, and a direct WhatsApp link.

### Task Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Not yet done |
| **Overdue** | Past due date, shown in red |
| **Completed** | Done ✅ |

Tasks due today are highlighted in **amber**. Completing a task sends a WhatsApp notification to the creator.

## 4. Feedback

Members submit service feedback via WhatsApp (`FEEDBACK` command). The Feedback tab shows:
- Average rating (1–5 stars)
- Category breakdown (Sermon / Worship / Welcome / General)
- Recent feedback list with comments, ratings, member names, and dates

---

## 5. Availability (Pastor's Schedule)

Configure when your pastor is available for appointments — used by the WhatsApp `BOOK` command.

1. Go to **Pastoral Care → Availability**
2. Click **+ Add Slot**
3. Set Day of Week, Start Time, End Time
4. Save

Each slot can be toggled on/off temporarily (e.g. when the pastor is away).

## 6. Pastoral Visits

The **Visits tab** tracks all pastoral visits — home visits, hospital visits, bereavements, outreach, and more. It also surfaces **member-requested visits** from WhatsApp.

### Logging a Visit

1. Go to **Pastoral Care → Visits** → **+ Log a Visit**
2. Fill in: Member, Visited By, Visit Type, Date, Location (optional), Notes
3. Optionally tick **"Create a follow-up task"** to create a task in the same action
4. Click **Log Visit**

### Member-Requested Visits

When a member sends `VISIT` via WhatsApp, the request is logged with a **🙋 Requested** badge. Your team is notified via WhatsApp.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🙋 **Requested** | Member asked for a visit — not yet assigned |
| ✅ **Assigned** | A staff member has been assigned |
| *(no badge)* | Visit completed and logged |

### Visit Types

🏠 Home · 🏥 Hospital · 💜 Bereavement · 🌍 Outreach · 📋 Other

## 7. Bereavement & Crisis Care

The **Care Cases** tab tracks members going through bereavement, crisis, hospitalisation, or serious situations — with an automated 4-milestone care journey.

### Flagging a Member for Care

From the **member's profile**:
1. Click **💛 Flag for Care**
2. Select care type: Bereavement / Crisis / Hospitalisation / Family Crisis / Mental Health / General Care
3. Add optional notes → click **Flag & Notify Team**

Shepherd immediately sends a WhatsApp alert to all pastoral staff with the member's name, phone number, a one-tap WhatsApp link, and a link to their profile.

### The Automated Care Journey

| Milestone | When | Who Receives It |
|---|---|---|
| **Day 1 — Team notified** | Immediately | All pastoral staff |
| **Day 3 — Follow-up reminder** | 3 days after flagging | All pastoral staff |
| **Day 7 — Member check-in** | 7 days after flagging | The member directly |
| **Day 30 — Close-out prompt** | 30 days after flagging | All pastoral staff |

The Day 7 message goes to the member's WhatsApp in their language — a warm personal check-in.

### Closing a Care Case

Open the care case card → click **Close Case**. A member cannot be flagged again until their current case is closed.

## 8. Pastoral Care Analytics

The **Analytics tab** gives leadership a bird's-eye view of pastoral health:

- **Prayer:** Total requests, open/answered/closed counts, response rate, category breakdown, 6-month trend
- **Appointments:** Completion rate, type breakdown, monthly trend
- **Tasks:** Completion rate, overdue count, top assignees leaderboard
- **Care Cases:** Active vs. closed counts, care type breakdown, average days to closure

## Tips

- ✅ **Check the Tasks tab daily** — it's your team's to-do list
- ✅ **Mark prayers as "Praying" quickly** — it sends a warm WhatsApp to the member immediately
- ✅ **Set availability slots** before going live with WhatsApp booking
- ✅ **Use Care Notes** for sensitive conversations — not the regular member Notes field
